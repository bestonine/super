Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about VELOCITY CRASH.

Do I need to install anything?

No. VELOCITY CRASH is a browser-based game. It runs directly in modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, and Safari with no downloads or installations required.

What are the system requirements?

If your device can run a modern web browser, you can play. The game is optimized to run smoothly on a wide range of hardware, from high-end PCs to budget smartphones.

How is my score saved?

Your scores and progress are saved locally in your browser's storage. Clearing your browser's cache or site data will reset your progress.

What are the controls?

On desktop, use the Arrow Keys for steering, accelerating, and braking. On mobile, use the on-screen touch controls. Controls can be customized in the settings menu.

Is the game free to play?

Yes, VELOCITY CRASH is completely free to play. We may introduce optional cosmetic items in the future, but the core racing experience will always be free.
